What our freelance did

This was not a sales mission. It was a commercial architecture mission.

01

Found every crack in the process.

Ran interviews across Marketing, Sales and event teams to surface what was actually blocking deals, not the official version.

02

Built a system to rank every lead.

A 3-tier model to sort every contact by readiness: who to call today, who to nurture, and who to stop chasing.

03

Turned the salon into a revenue operation.

Pre-event playbook, role assignments, and a minimum of 3 booked appointments per salesperson per day.

04

Gave the team everything to close.

Finalized the product catalog, built the briefing kit, and designed the follow-up sequences so no one was improvising on the floor.

The result

The mission ended. The infrastructure stayed.

The mission diagnosed what was breaking the commercial process and delivered the infrastructure to fix it. Three areas rebuilt, usable from the next event forward.

Clarity

They know exactly what failed.

A documented diagnosis of every breakdown across Marketing, Sales and event operations. Root causes, not just symptoms.

System

The pipeline is sorted by readiness.

A model that routes every contact by readiness, from ready-to-close to long-term nurture. No longer one undifferentiated list.

Playbook

The next salon starts ready.

Pre-event outreach, referral activation, role assignments and a structured event plan — everything needed to run the next one independently.
